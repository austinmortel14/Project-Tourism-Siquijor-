About Beyond the Shore

Beyond the Shore in Siquijor is a serene beachfront resort offering a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. With comfortable accommodations, breathtaking ocean views, and a range of activities like diving, snorkeling, and nature walks, it’s an ideal destination for those seeking tranquility and connection with nature.

Siquijor

Siquijor is home to stunning natural wonders like the mystical Cambugahay Falls, the white-sand beaches of Salagdoong, the tranquil beauty of Siquijor’s Century-old Balete Tree, and the peaceful sanctuary of the Cathedral of St. Francis of Assisi, offering a perfect mix of adventure and serenity.
