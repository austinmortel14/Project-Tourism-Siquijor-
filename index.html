Beyond the Shore in Siquijor offers beachfront access, comfortable rooms with modern amenities, and a peaceful environment. Enjoy activities like diving, snorkeling, yoga, and relaxing massage services. There’s also a restaurant, Wi-Fi, and a boutique for souvenirs.

Connect with Nature

Experience the beauty of Siquijor by connecting with nature through guided nature walks. Explore lush forests, vibrant flora, and scenic landscapes, while immersing yourself in the island’s serene and peaceful environment. Perfect for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility.

Meet the Sea Buddies

Meet the Sea Buddies at Beyond the Shore in Siquijor, where you can dive into vibrant underwater worlds.
